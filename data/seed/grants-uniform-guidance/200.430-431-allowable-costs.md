# §§ 200.430–200.431 — Compensation & Fringe (allowable costs, starter stub)

**§ 200.430 Compensation — personal services.** Costs of compensation are
allowable to the extent that they satisfy the specific requirements of this
part, and that the total compensation for individual employees is reasonable for
the services rendered, conforms to the established written policy of the
non-Federal entity consistently applied, and is supported by adequate
documentation (records that reflect the total activity for which the employee is
compensated).

**§ 200.431 Compensation — fringe benefits.** Fringe benefits are allowances and
services provided by employers to their employees as compensation in addition to
regular salaries and wages. Fringe benefits (such as leave, employee insurance,
pensions, and unemployment benefit plans) are allowable provided they are
reasonable and are required by law, employer-employee agreement, or an
established policy of the non-Federal entity.
