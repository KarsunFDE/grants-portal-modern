# § 200.211 — Information Contained in a Federal Award (starter stub)

A Federal award must include the following general information about the award:
recipient name and Unique Entity Identifier (UEI); the Federal Award
Identification Number (FAIN); the Federal award date; the period of performance
start and end dates; the budget period start and end dates; the total amount of
Federal funds obligated; and the Assistance Listing Number and program title.

The award must also identify the funding instrument (grant or cooperative
agreement), the awarding agency contact information, any indirect cost rate, and
the general terms and conditions — including the requirement that the recipient
comply with the Uniform Guidance (2 CFR 200) and program-specific terms.
