# §§ 200.328–200.329 — Post-Award Reporting (starter stub)

**§ 200.328 Financial reporting.** The Federal awarding agency must require
recipients to use the OMB-approved governmentwide standard information
collection (e.g., the Federal Financial Report, SF-425) when financial reports
are required. The agency must not impose more frequent reporting than annually
unless specific conditions warrant; interim reports may be required quarterly or
semiannually.

**§ 200.329 Monitoring and reporting program performance.** The recipient is
responsible for oversight of the operations of the Federal award-supported
activities and must monitor its activities to assure compliance and that
performance goals are being achieved. Performance reports must compare actual
accomplishments to the objectives established for the period, and explain why
established goals were not met when applicable. Reporting frequency is set in
the terms and conditions (commonly annual, with a final report at closeout).
