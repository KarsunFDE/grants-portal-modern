# 2 CFR 200 Subpart A — Key Definitions (starter stub)

**Recipient (§ 200.1)** — an entity, usually but not limited to a non-Federal
entity, that receives a Federal award directly from a Federal awarding agency.
The term does not include subrecipients or individuals that are beneficiaries
of the award.

**Federal award (§ 200.1)** — the Federal financial assistance that a recipient
receives directly from a Federal awarding agency or indirectly from a
pass-through entity. It does not include payments to a contractor or payments to
an individual that is a beneficiary of a Federal program.

**Period of performance (§ 200.1)** — the total estimated time interval between
the start of an initial Federal award and the planned end date, which may
include one or more funded portions, or budget periods.

**Assistance Listing (§ 200.1)** — a listing of all Federal financial
assistance programs published by the Office of Management and Budget at
SAM.gov (formerly the Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance, "CFDA"); each
program is identified by an Assistance Listing Number (ALN).
