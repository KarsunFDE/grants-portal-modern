# SF-424 "Application for Federal Assistance" — Field Reference (starter stub)

The SF-424 is the governmentwide standard application cover form for federal
grants and cooperative agreements. Key fields used by this application's intake
wizard:

- **Type of Submission / Type of Application** — preapplication, application, or
  changed/corrected application; new, continuation, or revision.
- **Funding Opportunity Number** — the NOFO number from the announcement.
- **Assistance Listing Number (CFDA/ALN) and Title** — program identifier.
- **Applicant (Item 8)** — legal name, address, Employer/Taxpayer ID, and
  **Unique Entity Identifier (UEI)** (the 12-character SAM identifier that
  replaced DUNS in April 2022).
- **Type of Applicant (Item 9)** — coded category (state, county, city,
  nonprofit, institution of higher education, individual, for-profit, etc.).
- **Descriptive Title of Applicant's Project (Item 15)** — project title.
- **Areas Affected by Project (Item 14)** — geographic scope.
- **Estimated Funding (Item 18)** — federal, applicant/match, and total amounts.
- **Project Director / Principal Investigator (Item 21)** — contact for the
  technical work.

> Starter stub — abbreviated field list. See the SF-424 form package on
> Grants.gov for the authoritative form and instructions.
